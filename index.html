About

Yoku Taberu means to 'Eat Well' in Japanese. Yoku Taberu was established in 2008. It is our mission at Yoku Taberu to empower people around the world to eat well. We celebrate the simplicity and beauty of Japanese food and want you all to share in our passion.

Learn how to prepare and cook authentic traditional Japanese cuisine from the comfort of your own kitchen. At Yoku Taberu we will teach you the techniques to perfect your skills in all aspects of Japanese dining.

From Sushi rolling to sauce making and everything in-between. All lessons are delivered through a live zoom session on a weekly basis. By some of the world's top international professional chefs. To get more information fill out the contact form and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
